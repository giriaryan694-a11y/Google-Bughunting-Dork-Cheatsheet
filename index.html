üí° PRO TIPS FOR EFFECTIVE DORKING

Combine Operators: Chain operators together to refine results. Example: site:example.com intext:"API_KEY" -inurl:github.
Exclude Clutter: Use the minus sign (-) to filter out unwanted results. Example: inurl:login -inurl:assets.
Search Exact Phrases: Use quotes (" ") to find specific strings like "DB_PASSWORD=".
Target Specific Sites: Use site: to focus on platforms like site:pastebin.com or site:github.com where secrets are sometimes leaked.
Leverage the GHDB: For inspiration and a massive collection of tested dorks, refer to the Exploit Database's Google Hacking Database (GHDB).
